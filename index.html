The Problem

Most business owners we talk to are losing money in ways they didn’t even know about.

01

Missed calls = lost customers

Every call that hits voicemail is a real person who needed help. They don’t leave a message — they call the next business and book with them. You never even know it happened.

02

Leads go cold in minutes

There’s a 5-minute window to respond before a lead goes cold. Most businesses get back to them hours later — if at all. By then they’ve already moved on.

03

No-shows block your schedule

A no-show isn’t just annoying — it’s a slot that could’ve gone to someone who actually wanted it. We’ve seen businesses losing multiple bookings a week just from this alone.
